MeMovies

Welcome to our movie tracking website! Here, you can easily keep track of all the movies you have watched and want to watch. Adding a movie to your list is as simple as searching for it by title and clicking "Add to list". You can then view your entire list of movies by visiting the "My Movies" page, where you can also filter your movies by genre to easily find what you're looking for. If you want to make any changes to a movie in your list, such as marking it as watched or updating its notes, you can do so by clicking on the movie and editing its details. If you decide you no longer want to keep a movie in your list, you can delete it with just a few clicks. With our website, keeping track of your movie journey and discovering new films has never been easier!
